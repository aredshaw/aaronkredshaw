Installing a Sound Card

Follow these steps to install your sound card

  1. Put on your anti-static strap or touch the metal case of the computer.
  2. Find an open PCI slot on your computer.
  3. Remove the I/O shield so you will have access to the card once the computer is all put together.
  4. Insert the sound card, pushing on one end first and then moving your way to the other side.
