Finishing Up

Your computer is just about done. Here is what you need to do to finish up.

  1. If you have any other cards or devices to install, do that.
  2. Make sure hard drives and CD/DVD drives are screwed in correctly.
  3. Make sure all devices are connected with the correct cables and they all fit snugly.
  4. Put your case back together with the correct screws.
  5. Install your Windows OS and any other software you want on your new computer.
  6. Bask in the fact that you just built your own computer!
