Installing a Graphics Card

Graphics card installation is a snap. Here is how you do it.

  1. First put on your anti-static strap or touch the metal computer case before starting.
  2. The interface for a graphics card is a either an AGP (older) or PCI. Find the slot where your graphics card fits.
  3. Make sure to remove the I/O shield (making it accessible to plug into when closing up the case)
